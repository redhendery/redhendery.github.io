About Me

As a recent graduate of the Enspiral Dev Academy, I am looking for junior developer roles in an environment where I am able to continue to learn my craft. Ideally I will find an employer who fosters my desire to continue learning and developing on a personal level.

I have strong background in customer support and sales. Having worked in numerous customer service roles, sales positions and on technical support helpdesks I have developed exceptional communication and soft skills. I thrive in a team environment where I contribute through my hard work ethic, attention to detail, positive attitude and clear coherent communication. These are ways I feel I can immediately contribute to a business from day one.

I believe in Test Driven Development (TDD) and Red, Green, Refactor as a principal for coding. A theory I applied to writing my Resume, “refactoring” it several times.

With experience working in an Agile environment, I have seen the advantages of working in this way. I firmly believe that regular communication between team members helps everyone stay on the same page and the willingness to move in different directions is key to delivering on quality projects.
